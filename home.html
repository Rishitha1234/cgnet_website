ONGOING PROJECT

Stepping into a new year is an occasion of reminiscing and celebrating new possibilities with your loved ones. This new year, let’s extend our love to our sisters fighting strong in Bastar.

After having borne the brunt of the decades old Maoist insurgency, the beautiful district of Bastar, with its sprawling forests and vibrant culture, has seen large scale devastation and violence. And so, its people became victims, from experiencing the tragic loss of family members and homes to displacement and abuse.
